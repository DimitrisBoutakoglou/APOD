Curious about Curiosity Mars Rover?

Curiosity is a car-sized Mars rover exploring Gale crater and Mount Sharp on Mars as part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory mission. Curiosity was launched from Cape Canaveral on November 26, 2011, and landed on Aeolis Palus inside Gale crater on Mars on August 6, 2012.
